# Copyright Assignment Agreement

This Copyright Assignment Agreement is intended for contributors whose contributions are accepted subject to express assignment rather than, or in addition to, the Contributor License Agreement.

By signing or otherwise expressly agreeing to this agreement in writing, the contributor agrees as follows:

## 1. Assigned Materials

"Assigned Contribution" means any code, documentation, design, test, script, asset, or other material identified in the applicable signature block, email confirmation, pull request confirmation, or other written instrument referencing this agreement.

## 2. Assignment

The contributor irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Callum, and to any successor, assignee, or designated receiving entity identified by Callum in writing, all right, title, and interest, including all copyrights and copyright renewals, in and to the Assigned Contribution to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

## 3. Further Assurances

The contributor agrees to execute additional documents and take reasonable actions requested by the receiving party to confirm, perfect, record, or memorialize the assignment under this agreement.

## 4. Moral Rights and Waivers

To the extent permitted by applicable law, the contributor waives, and agrees not to assert, any moral rights, droit moral, or similar rights that would interfere with the exercise of the assigned rights. If complete waiver is not permitted, the contributor grants all consents necessary for the receiving party and its successors, assigns, licensees, and sublicensees to exercise the assigned rights without further consent.

## 5. Contributor Representations

The contributor represents and warrants that:

- the Assigned Contribution is original to the contributor, or the contributor has sufficient rights to assign it
- the contributor has obtained any required employer, client, institutional, or third-party consents
- the Assigned Contribution does not intentionally infringe the rights of any third party
- the contributor has full authority to enter into this agreement

## 6. No Compensation Unless Separately Agreed

Unless expressly stated in a separate written agreement signed by the receiving party, the assignment does not create any right to royalties, revenue sharing, profit sharing, equity, compensation, reimbursement, or continuing approval rights.

## 7. Commercialization and Transfer

The receiving party may use, modify, license, sublicense, relicense, commercialize, dual-license, open-source, close-source, transfer, assign, or otherwise exploit the Assigned Contribution, and may transfer it to any current or future company, affiliate, successor, acquirer, investor-backed entity, or other designated entity.

## 8. Priority of Signed Instruments

This file is a template notice. A separate written instrument, signature workflow, or written acceptance referencing this agreement should identify the contributor and the Assigned Contribution and will control the specific assignment transaction.

## 9. No Legal Advice

Contributors are responsible for obtaining their own legal advice before signing any assignment.
