# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Favewise.

## Before You Contribute

Before opening an Issue, Pull Request, or submitting any other form of contribution, please make sure your proposed changes are relevant to the project direction and clearly describe the purpose, implementation approach, and expected impact of the change.

By submitting a contribution for review, you acknowledge that the Project is intended to support current or future commercial, paid, hosted, enterprise, financed, transferred, or otherwise monetized offerings.

See [LEGAL-SIGNOFF.md](./LEGAL-SIGNOFF.md) for the current contribution approval workflow.

See [LICENSE](./LICENSE) for the current project license.

In addition, contributors may be required to agree to the terms of [CLA.md](./CLA.md) and, for certain contributions, [COPYRIGHT-ASSIGNMENT.md](./COPYRIGHT-ASSIGNMENT.md) before a contribution is accepted, merged, or used.

If requested by the maintainers, contributors may also be asked to complete the confirmation format described in [CONTRIBUTOR-SIGNATURE-TEMPLATE.md](./CONTRIBUTOR-SIGNATURE-TEMPLATE.md) or a separate written instrument.

## Contributor Representations

By submitting a contribution, you represent that:

- you have the necessary rights or authorization to submit the contribution

- you understand that, once merged, your contribution may be publicly distributed, modified, and used in future versions of the project in accordance with the project's applicable license terms and any separate written agreement, if any

## Development Notes

- run the project locally before submitting, and confirm it still starts correctly
- keep changes focused and avoid mixing unrelated edits in the same PR
- open an Issue first if your proposed change is large or affects project direction

## Conduct

Please participate in a respectful, clear, and constructive way.
